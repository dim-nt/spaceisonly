Two - story house / Thessaloniki,GR / 180 sqm | COMPLETED 2023

This project involved the complete renovation and redesign of a classic two-story residence. Our approach focused on maximizing natural light, creating open, flowing spaces, and integrating contemporary design elements while respecting the home's original character. Key features include a custom-designed kitchen, a serene master suite, and a seamless connection between indoor and outdoor living areas.

Materials were carefully selected for their durability, beauty, and sustainability. The color palette is a sophisticated blend of neutral tones with strategic accents, creating a warm and inviting atmosphere throughout the home.
